Biography

Kwesi Brew was born in 1928 in Cape Coast, Ghana. A writer and a diplomat, he is the author of critically acclaimed poetry collections such as The Shadows of Laughter (1968), African Panorama and Other Poems (Greenfield Center, New York, 1981), Return of No Return and other poems (1995), The Clan of the Leopard and other poems (1996). He attended the University College of the Gold Coast (now Ghana) and earned a degree in English in 1953. The winner of a British Council Poetry Competition in Accra, Brew’s work has been published in many anthologies across the world.
