Biography

Stephen Watson was born in Cape Town in 1954. He is the author of nine collections of poetry and three books of non-fiction, many of which were focused on the city of Cape Town. He attended the University of Cape Town as an undergraduate student to study English. He also received a master's and doctorate from the same university and went on to work as a professor in the English department at that institution.
