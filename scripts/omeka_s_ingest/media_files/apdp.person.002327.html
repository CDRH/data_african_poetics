Biography

Kolanyane-Kesupile was born in January 1988 in Francistown. She is one of the first transgender people to come out openly in Botswana. She received a bachelor's degree in drama and dramatics from the University of the Witwatersrand and earned a master's degree in human rights, culture and social justice from Goldsmiths, University of London where she was Chevening Scholar in 2016. She is the founder of the Queer Shorts Showcase Festival, which is the first and only LGBT themed theater festival in Botswana. She has also written for Peolwane Magazine, The Kalahari Review, The Washington Blade, and AfroPUNK.com.
