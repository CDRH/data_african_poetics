Biography

Mohammed Ebnu was born in the Western Sahara in 1968. He is most well known for his Spanish language poetry collections 'Voz de fuego' and 'Nómada en el Exilio'. He studied literature at the Instituto Superior Pedagógico de Pinar del Río, Cuba, and went on to become a founding member of the Saharawi Friendship Generation, a group dedicated to the advancement of Saharawi writers and literature. He has contributed extensively to Spanish anthologies such as Añoranza, Bubisher, Aaiún, gritando lo que se siente, Thirty one, and El azri de la badia saharaui. He currently lives in Spain.
