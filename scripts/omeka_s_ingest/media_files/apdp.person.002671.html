Biography

Bayo Adebowale was born in Adeyipo Village, Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria, where he will eventually open the African Heritage Library and Cultural Centre later in life as a librarian. Mostly known as a novelist, he is also a writer of short stories and poetry. Beyond writing, he is a literary critic and scholar. His most notable novel is called, The Virgin, which has been turned into a film. His poems can be found in his collection, A Night of Incantations (and Other Poems). He is currently a professor of Creative Writing.
