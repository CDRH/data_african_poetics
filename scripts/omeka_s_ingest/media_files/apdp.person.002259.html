Biography

Boujema El Aoufi was born in 1961 in Taza, Morocco. He began writing in 1986 and went on publish two collections of poetry Bayadat Shayiqa and Asdiqa’ Yughadiruna Hunjurati, characterized by their ordinary language and concern for everyday life. He is a member of both the Union of Moroccan Writers and the House of Poetry in Morocco. He is also the recipient of the 2001 Shariqa Award for Arab Creative Writing offered by the Ministry of Culture and Communication in the United Arab Emirates. He organizes poetic events in Morocco designed to promote the country’s poetry.
