Biography

Mbuh Tennu Mbuh was born in Pinyin, Cameroon. He is the author of several books including In the Shadow of My Country, The Oracle of Tears, and A Name That is Mine. He attended the University of Yaoundé where he obtained a bachelor's degree in English Literary Studies. He continued on to earn a doctorate from the same university and another doctorate degree from the University of Nottingham. He is a two-time laureate of the Bernard Fonlon Society Literary Award, and a founding member of the Yaoundé University Poetry Club and of the Anglophone Cameroon Writers Association. He went on to become a professor in the department of English Studies at the University of Yaoundé.
