Biography

Abdel-ilah Salhi was born in 1968 in Beni Mellal, Morocco. He has a bachelor's degree in English and a master of arts degree in English literature. In France, where he moved in 1990, he explored the French literary scene and became acquainted with many Arab expatriate writers, including Iraqi poet Abdelkader Al Janabi. Through him, he frequented the Arab surrealist circle in Paris. This inspired him to began his own literary magazine Israf (Excess), which had a significant impact on the prose poem and surrealism in Morocco. He works as a journalist and radio correspondent in France.
