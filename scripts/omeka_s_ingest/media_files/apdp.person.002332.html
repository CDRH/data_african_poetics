Biography

Mbongeni Khumalo was born in 1976 in South Africa. He is the author of two poetry collections, Apocrypha and Throbbing Ink. He is also the recipient of a merit award from the English Academy of South Africa. In addition to his books of poetry, his poems have been published in New Coin, Global Fire, Tribute, Timbila and Botsotso. A performance artist, he regularly performs his poetry in his native South Africa.
