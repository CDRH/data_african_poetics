Biography

Ahmad Al Shahawi was born in 1960 in Damietta, Egypt. He attended Assiut University where he studied journalism. He went on to work at Al-Ahram newspaper before becoming the editor-in-chief of the weekly magazine "Nisf Al-Dunya." He is the author of several poetry collections including: wo Rak'at for Love, Book of Death and Say That It Is Her. He won the UNESCO Prize in Literature in 1995 and the Cavafy Poetry Prize in 1998. His work has been translated into many languages including English, Dutch, French, Spanish, and Turkish.
