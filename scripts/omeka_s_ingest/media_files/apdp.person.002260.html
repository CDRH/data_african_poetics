Biography

Osama al-Danasuri was born in Disuq in the Nile Delta. He studied maritime sciences at the University of Ismailia in 1984. He went on to write several books of poetry including: Hareshefo al-gahme (Scales of the sullen) and Methlu dhe'ben a'ma (Like a Blind Wolf). He is considered one of the greatest Egyptian cultivators of the prose poem and one of the most important Arab poets. He passed away on January 4th, 2007 and was buried in his hometown. His only narrative book, Kalbeya al-harem, kalbeya al-habib, was published posthumously to great success.
