Biography

Haji Gora Haji was born in 1933 in Tumbatu, Zanzibar. He is best known for Kimbunga, his debut poetry collection, published in 1994. He never received a formal education but learned the alphabet from a childhood friend. He attended a madrasa as a child where he studied the Koran. His publications include several books of poems, such as Utenzi wa Visa vya Nabii Suleiman bin Daudi (The Epic of Prophet Suleiman, Son of Daud) and Siri ya Ging’ingi (The Secrets of Ging’ingi), along with five children’s books and a novel.
