Biography

Jamal Gabobe was born in Hargeisa, Somaliland. He earned his a Ph.D (Comparative Literature) from the University of Washington. He has published a book of poems ‘Love and Memory’ and an essay in the anthology ‘An Ear to the Ground: Presenting Writers from 2 Coasts.’ (2021). He was part of the Jackstraw Writers Program and has written on modern Egyptian literature and the Canadian tutor Magaret Laurence. He is currently an instructor/consultant at the University of Washington’s Center for Teaching and Learning.
