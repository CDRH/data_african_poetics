Biography

Buma Kor is a poet from Cameroon. He is most well known for his book of poetry Searchlight Poems. He has a masters in business administration and publishing from Oxford Brookes University. He organized several writing and artist workshops and provided a platform for writers on a radio program entitled the Young Writers Forum. As a result of these, he compiled the first Anglophone Cameroon Writers anthology which was exhibited at the first Agro-pastoral show in 1973. With very few foreign publishers of Anglophone Cameroonian writing, Kor started his own publishing company in 1974. He went on to work as Publishing Co-ordinator at the UNESCO Centre for Book Promotion in Africa.
