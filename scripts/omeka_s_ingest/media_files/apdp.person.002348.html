Biography

Irmagard Anchang Langmia is a Cameroonian poet and teacher. She is the author of the poetry collection Visions in My Mind's Eye which explored a multitude of cultural experiences rooted in everyday, routine life at home in her native Cameroon and abroad. She has a bachelor's and a master's degree from the University of Yaounde and is currently pursuing a doctoral degree from Northcentral University. She taught for many years at Bowie State University. She lives in Maryland, United States.
