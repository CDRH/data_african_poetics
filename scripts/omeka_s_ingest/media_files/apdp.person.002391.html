Biography

Pinkie Mekgwe was born in Botswana. She went to the University of Botswana where she received a Bachelor of Arts. She went on to obtain a Master's degree in Critical Theory and a Doctor of Philosophy degree in Gender and Literary Studies at the University of Sussex. In addition to writing poetry, she has served as an assistant lecturer at Sussex University’s School of African and Asian Studies, and a visiting lecturer at Malmo University in Sweden. She has also worked in international development in various contexts and as a producer and presenter of “Open Book,” an educational, literature-focused radio programme aired on a Gaborone-based radio station.
