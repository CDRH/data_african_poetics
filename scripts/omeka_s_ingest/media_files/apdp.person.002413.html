Biography

Nick Mulgrew was born in 1990 in Durban, South Africa. He is most well known as a poet for his collection "the myth of this is that we’re all in this together," which explores the ways in which people lose trust in each other, their communities, and themselves. He went to Rhodes University for his Bachelor of Arts in English and Journalism & Media Studies; University of Cape Town for his Bachelor of Arts in Media Theory & Practice and his MA in English Language & Literature; and is currently pursuing a Doctor of Philosophy in Writing Practice at the University of Dundee in Scotland. He was shortlisted for the National Poetry Prize, South Africa, in 2022. He founded and directs the poetry press uHlanga.
