Biography

Len Verwey was born in Mozambique in 1973. He is the author of two full-length collections of poetry, Loving the Dying and In a Language That You Know, and a poetry chapbook, Otherwise Everything Goes On, which was included in the box set Seven New Generation African Poets published by the African Poetry Book Fund. He is the recipient of a master's degree in comparative literature as well as a degree in commerce from UNISA and Stellenbosch, respectively. In addition to his poetic work, he has worked as an economist, researcher, and analyst in areas such as public finance, governance, and climate change.
