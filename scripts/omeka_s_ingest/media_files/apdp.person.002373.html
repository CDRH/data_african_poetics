Biography

Albert G.T.K. Malikongwa was born in 1930 in Mayebe Nswazwi, Botswana. He is the author of the poetry collection The Smell of Cowdung and the play Chief Mengwe IV. He attended St. Josephâ€™s College and Roma College, and later received a degree in public administration from the University of York. He worked as a teacher for several years before continuing on to work for the government, retiring as a member of the Customary Court of Appeal. He received the Presidential Award for Meritorious Service in 1999 from former president of Botswana, Festus Mogae.
