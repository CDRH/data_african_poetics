Biography

Clinton V du Plessis was born in 1963 in Eastern Cape, South Africa. He is the author of several books of poetry, including Confession of a Hermit, Curriculum Vitae, Words Rust in the Water, and In God's country: no. 2–21. Although he never completed his degree, he attended the University of South Africa. He went on to work as an accountant for many years while writing and publishing books of poetry. To aid up-and-coming writers, in 1987 he founded his own publishing house and literary magazine Akuut. His work has been published in English, Afrikaans, and Spanish.
