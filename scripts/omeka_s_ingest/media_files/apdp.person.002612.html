Biography

Akol Miyen Kuol, commonly known as Akoldit, was born in 1974 in Thurkuruk Village in South Sudan. He fled from the region in 1978 due to political instability and has lived in exile since 1993. A poet and journalist, Kuol has published two collections of poetry. The frirst was "The Sun Will be Rising" in 2001 and the second, "The Last Train" in 2003. His poetry explores the violence of the War in Sudan and his longing for reconciliation between its people. Kuol was educated in Egypt and northern Sudan. His journalistic work has included reporting for the BBC and the Sudan Mirror on Sudan, the Middle east, and the Sahel. Kuol currently lives in Kenya.
