Biography

Safia Elhillo was born in 1990 in Rockville, Maryland. A Sudanese by way of Washington DC, Elhillo is an award-winning poet and the author of poetry books such as The Life and Times of Susie Knuckles (2012), ars poetica (2016), a suite for ol' dirty (2016) Asmarani (2016), The January Children (2017), Home Is Not A Country (2021), and Girls That Never Die (2022). Elhillo earned her B.A. at Gallatin School, New York University, and her MFA in Poetry at The New School. She received the Sillerman First Book Prize for African Poets and the Arab American Book Award in 2015 and 2018 respectively.
