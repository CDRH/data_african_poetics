Biography

Emmanuel Laugier was born in 1969 in Meknes, Morocco. He is the author of several books of French language poetry, including: For, Portrait de têtes, Et je suis hors déjà je suis dans l'air, and The eye band. He studied philosophy in Nice and at the Sorbonne in Paris. He went on to work as an essayist and literary critic for the newspaper Le Matricule des Anges for many years, and has served as the editor of critical anthologies on contemporary poetry. He was a member and grant recipient of the “Poetry Commission” of the Center National du Livre.
