Biography

Ehab Khalifa was born in Tahta, Sohag, Upper Egypt. He studied English Language and Literature and went on to become an English teacher at several international schools in Egypt. He also attended the American University School of Continuing Studies where he received professional certificates which allowed him to continue his work as a teacher. Although he is yet to publish a full length collection of poetry, his poems have appeared in several avenues. He frequently works as a translator and researcher.
