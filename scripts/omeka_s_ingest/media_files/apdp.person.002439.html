Biography

Mostafa Nissaboury was born in 1943 in Casablanca, Morocco. He has published several books of poetry including Approche du désertique précédé de Aube and La mille et deuxième nuit. He began his university studies, but eventually had to abandon them for employment. In 1964, he published a manifesto arguing for the rethinking and modernization of Moroccan poetry, along with Mohammed Khair-Eddine. He was one of the founders of the review Souffles and is a member of the House of Poetry in Morocco.
