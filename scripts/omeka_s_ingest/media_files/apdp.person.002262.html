Biography

Gamal Elgezeery was born in 1973 in Egypt. An award-winning poet, storywriter, novelist, critic, translator and academic, he has published 55 books in Arabic and English since 2001, including novels, poetry volumes, story collections, literary criticism, and translations. He is currently an associate professor of English literature at Taibah University, KSA. He teaches English literature and language courses at Suez University (Egypt) and Taibah University (Saudi Arabia).
