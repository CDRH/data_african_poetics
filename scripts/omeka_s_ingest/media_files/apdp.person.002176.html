Biography

Nol Alembong is a scholar, poet, and prose writer from Cameroon. He is the author of three collections of poetry: Forest Echos (MiraClaire Publishing 2012), Passing Winds (2013, MiraClaire Publishing), and Green Call (2017, Notion Press). He also published the short story “Here, Where We Live” in Calalloo in 1980. He has taught as a Professor of African literature at the University of Yaounde I in Cameroon, and currently, serves as the Dean of the Faculty of Arts at the University of Buea, Cameroon.
