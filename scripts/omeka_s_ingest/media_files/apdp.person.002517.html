Biography

Dibussi Tande was born in 1968 in Buea, Cameroon. He is a political scientist, writer and poet who lives in Chicago. He obtained a “Licence” in Public law (1989) and a “Maitrise” in Political Science (1990) from the University of Yaounde. He also holds Master’s degrees in Political Science and Instructional Technology from Northeastern Illinois University and Northern Illinois University respectively. His poems have been published in several anthologies. His collections include No Turning Back: Poems of Freedom 1990-1993 (2007), Bearing Witness: Poems from a Land in Turmoil (2020) by Joyce Ashuntantang and Dibussi Tande, Their Champagne Party Will End! Poems in Honor of Bate Besong by Ashuntantang and Dibussi Tande (2008).
