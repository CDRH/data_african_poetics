Biography

Ike Mboneni Muila was born 1955 in Soweto, South Africa. He is most well known for his work with the poetry performance group, the Botsotso Jesters, with whom he has published recordings and performed at festivals. Between 1988 and 1990, Muila performed with the Madimba music students at the Soyinkwa Institute of African Theatre in Soweto. His recent solo collection, GOVA, includes drawings as well as recordings, and reflects the multilingual, many-faceted life of South Africa. Muila continued as a performance poet with the Botsotso Jesters, and serves as Board Chair of Botsotso Publishing, a publishing, dramatic arts, literary management, and activist organization of which he was a founding member.
