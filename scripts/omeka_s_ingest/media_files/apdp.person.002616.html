Biography

Chris Abani was born in Afikpo, Ebonyi State, Nigeria in 1966. He is an accomplished novelist, poet, essayist, screenwriter, and playwright. He has published eight collections of poetry such as Kalakuta Republic, Hands Washing Water, and Smoking the Bible. According to the Poetry Foundation, "Abani began writing at a very young age and published his first novel, Masters of the Board (1985), while still a teenager." Among his accolades are the PEN USA West Freedom-to-Write Award and the Imbongi Yesizwe Poetry International Award. He is currently Board of Trustees Professor of English at Northwestern University.
