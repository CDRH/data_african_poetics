Biography

Mohammed Bentalha was born in Fez, Morocco in 1950. He is the author of three collections of poems. He was one of the founders of the House of Morocco and the Moroccan League of Writers. He was also the general secretary of the Moroccan Union of Writers for several years. He is a professor of literature and chair of the department of Arabic at the Ecole Normale Superieure Marrakech.
