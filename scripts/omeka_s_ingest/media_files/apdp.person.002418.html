Biography

Sandra A. Mushi was born 1984 in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. She is most well known for her collection of short stories and poems, Stains on My Khanga, published in 2014, which explores the lives of women, frequently dealing with oppressive situations and domestic violence. She grew up in the UK and was educated in Tanzania, Botswana, and South Africa. She studied Interior Design at Cape Peninsula University of Technology in Cape Town, and continued on to a career in interior design and architecture design. She is now a practicing interior designer and writer.
