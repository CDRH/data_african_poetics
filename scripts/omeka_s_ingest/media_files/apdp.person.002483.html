Biography

Arja Salafranca was born in 1971 in MÃ¡laga, Spain to a Spanish father and South African mother. She is the author of three poetry collections: A Life Stripped of Illusions, The Fire in Which We Burn, and Beyond Touch. Her debut collection was awarded the Sanlam Prize and most recollection was co-winner of the South African Literary award. She attended the University of Witwatersrand where she obtained a bachelor's degree and a master's degree in creative writing. She has served as the lifestyle editor at The Sunday Independent. She lives in Johannesburg.
