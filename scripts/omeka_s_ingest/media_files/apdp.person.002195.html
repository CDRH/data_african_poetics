Biography

Shabbir Banoobhai was born to Indian parents in 1949 in Saville Street located in the city of Durban. He has published many poetry collections including echoes of my other self (1980), shadows of a sun-darkened land (1984), lyrics to paradise (2004), and the drums beat all night (2013) in which he explores spirituality and celebrates divinity in humanity. He went to the University of South Africa (Unisa) for his Bachelor of Commerce Degree. Ranked among South Africa’s most acclaimed poets, Banoobhai has successfully established himself as an accountant, a political activist and a spiritual thought leader as he continues to write reflections on love, leadership, and philosophy.
