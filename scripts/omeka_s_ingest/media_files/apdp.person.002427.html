Biography

Mvula ya Nangolo was born in 1943 in Oniimwandi, Namibia. He joined the independence movement SWAPO at the age of eighteen before receiving a journalism scholarship to study in Germany. One of the first black journalists in Namibia, he was the first editor of Namibia Today and worked as a Special Advisor to the Ministry of Information and Communication Technology. For his poetry, he was awarded the position of National Poet of Namibia. He died in 2019 in Windhoek, Namibia. His poetry publications include From Exile, Thoughts from Exile, and Watering the Beloved Desert.
