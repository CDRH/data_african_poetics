Biography

Keamogetsi Joseph Molapong was born 1972 in Windhoek, Namibia. Both a print and performance poet, he has contributed to several poetry anthologies such as Come Talk Your Heart, Poetically Speaking, and In Search of Questions. He has also performed at shows such as The Black People I Know and The Scars on My Skin Part 1.He was a founding member of the "Kitso Poets," a group devoted to practising and teaching performance poetry as a way of educating the population and combating illiteracy. Through his company, Township Productions, he has produced several poetry events, organised regional poetry festivals and high school poetry competitions, and published Poetree, a series of poetry publications.
