Biography

Refiloe Mabejane (née Letokoto) was born in South Africa. She went to the University of Johannesburg where she studied psychology. She also received a Master's in International Development at the University of East Anglia in the UK. In addition to her performance poetry, ficition, and creative non-fiction, she works as an professional in international development, specializing in gender issues. She is a trustee and founding member of the non-profit organization ‘Family Art and Literacy Centre.’
