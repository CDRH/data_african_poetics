Biography

Mohamed Bachkar was born in 1969 in Rabat, Morocco. He is the author of two books of poetry: Mala'ika fi masahhat al-jahim and Khabtu tayr. Seminal Moroccan critic Najib Elaoufi said that Bachkar “approaches poetry as a special agnostic and mystical realm, as a case of mysterious attraction and rapture.” Bachkar worked as a journalist for many years, writing a popular weekly column in Al-Alam newspaper. He is a member of both the Union of Moroccan Writers and the House of Poetry in Morocco.
