Biography

Charl-Pierre Naudé was born in 1958 in kwaZulu Natal, South Africa. He went to the University of Stellenbosch where he studied literature and philosophy. He went on to work as a journalist. He is the author of three books of poetry, Die Nomadiese Oomblik (The Nomadic Moment), In Die Geheim van Die Dag (Against the Light) and Al Die Lieflike Dade (All Those Lovely Acts). Poetry International in Rotterdam devoted a translation project to his work, and several translations of his poems have appeared in both Dutch and Flemish magazines. He currently lives in Johannesburg.
