Biography

Taban Lo Liyong was born in Kajo Kaji, Acholiland, South Sudan, but grew up in Uganda. He is a prolific writer with over thirty books published in poetry, fiction, and short stories. He is also a playwright where he wrote Showhat and Sowhat in 2007. For his MFA, he attended the University of Iowa, where he became the first African writer to earn an MFA from their Creative Writing program. He taught at many universities in his lifetime with his last appointment was professor of Literature at Juba University in South Sudan that ended in 2020.
