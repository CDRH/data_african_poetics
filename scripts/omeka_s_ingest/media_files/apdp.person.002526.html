Biography

Hama Tuma was born in 1949 in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. He studied Law in Addis Ababa University but was expelled for demonstrating against the regime of Emperor Haile Selassie in his final year of law school. Subsequently, he was also banned by three different Ethiopian governments for his commitment to human rights. He left Ethiopia and travelled extensively before settling in France. He is the author of several books of poetry, many of which have been translated into other languages such as English, Italian, French, and Hebrew.
