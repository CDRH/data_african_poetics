Biography

Mahmoud Sharaf was born in Egypt. He graduated from Tanta University in Egypt in 1996. He has published several books of poetry, and is most well known for his collection Howwat Tabi'eya. His work has been published in Angry Voices: An Anthology of Off-beat New Egyptian Poets, Al-Qahirah, and Al-Kitaba al-Ohkra, among other publications. He is a journalist at Sout Al Arab radio station and the Nile Culture Channel. He also facilitates the annual Tanta International Festival.
