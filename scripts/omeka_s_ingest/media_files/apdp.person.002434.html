Biography

John Ngong Kum Ngong was born in Esu, Menchum, Cameroon. He attended Cameroon Protestant College Bali, Bilingual Grammar School Molyko in Buea, and the University of Yaound√©. He holds a master's degree in African Literature and a Certificate in Reading in a Foreign Language from the University of Birmingham. He is the author of two books of poetry, Walls of Agony and Chants of a Lunatic. He currently works as the principal of a high school.
