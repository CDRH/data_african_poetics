Biography

Limam Boicha was born in the Western Sahara in 1972. He is most well known for his Spanish language collection ‘Los versos de la madera.’ Forced to leave his home when he was nine due to the occupation of the Western Sahara region, he went on to study journalism at the Universidad de Oriente in Santiago de Cuba. He returned to the Sahara where he lived in refugee camps before going on to work at the Saharawi National Radio for several years. He later moved back to Spain where he lives and works in Barcelona.
