Biography

Tj Dema was born in 1981 in Gaborone, Botswana. A notable spoken word poet with years of experience performing poetry on different stages across the world, her poetry chapbook, Mandible (African Poetry Book Fund/Slapering Hol, USA, 2014) was included in the chapbook box set edited by Chris Abani and Kwame Dawes. In 2018, she was awarded the Sillerman First Book Prize for African Poets. Her forthcoming books include Meuterin (Parasitenpress, 2022), and an/another pastoral (No Bindings, 2022). She is an honorary research associate in the Department of English at the University of Bristol.
