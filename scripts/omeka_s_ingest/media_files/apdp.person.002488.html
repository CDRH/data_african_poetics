Biography

Barolong Seboni was born in 1957 in Kanye, Botswana. He went to the University of Botswana where he received a bachelor of arts degree and later attended the University of Wisconsinâ€“Madison where he obtained a master's degree. He continued on to become a senior lecturer and the director of the writers' workshop at the University of Botswana. He co-founded the Writers' Association of Botswana and also had a column in the Botswana Guardian. He is the author of several books of poetry.
