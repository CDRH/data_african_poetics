Biography

Bongekile Joyce Mbanjwa was born 1962 in Pietermaritzburg, South Africa. She is most well known for her collection Emotional Pain (Izinhlungu Zomphefumulo) published in Zulu and English. She attended the South African Council for Social Service Professions, University of Natal, KwaZulu-Natal Sign Language Academy and UNISA. She continued on to become a social services worker.
