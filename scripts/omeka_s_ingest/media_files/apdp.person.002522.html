Biography

Hamid Tibouchi was born in 1951 in Tibane, Algeria. He studied fine arts at the University of Paris VIII, and graduated in 1983. He worked as a French language assistant and English teacher before devoting himself to writing and producing works of visual arts. He is the author of over a dozen books of French language poems, including Mer Ouverte and Soleil d'herbe. As a visual artist, he has exhibited around sixty solo shows and nearly three hundred group exhibitions, in France and around the world.
