Biography

Christi N. Warner was born in Windhoek, Namibia to a Liberian mother. She has a master's degree in theatre for development from King Alfred's College in Winchester, UK. She is the author of the poetry collection Ice Cream and Politics. She has worked for non-governmental organisations, marrying her theatre background with her passion for furthering developmental projects. She also worked for the Namibian Broadcasting Corporation in a variety of roles. She has been invited to perform her poetry at the University of Namibia, various festivals, conferences, and official events in Namibia and abroad, including for the former First Lady at the Namibian State House.
