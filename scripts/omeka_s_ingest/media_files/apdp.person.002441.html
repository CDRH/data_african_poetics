Biography

Mutombo Nkulu-N’Sengha was born in the Democratic Republic of Congo. He is the author of Bela-Wenda, a book of poetry. He attended Major Seminar St. Paul where he obtained an undergraduate degree in Philosophy and Theology. He went on to receive two Master's degrees and a PhD. He currently teaches African religions, American religious diversity, and comparative religions at California State University. He also serves as the editor of the Congo Times. In addition to poetry, he has written extensively on Bumuntu philosophy.
