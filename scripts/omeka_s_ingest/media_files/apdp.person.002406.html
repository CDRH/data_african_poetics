Biography

Seitlhamo Motsapi was born in 1966 in Limpopo, South Africa. He is the author of Earthstepper/The Ocean is very Shallow in which he explored his social and political commitment in experimental forms. He attended the University of Witwatersrand where he pursued a Bachelor degree in English and Political Science and the University of Limpopo where he worked on a Bachelor of Arts (Honours) in English Studies. He is a former university lecturer and member of the President's office where he worked as a speechwriter. His work has appeared in numerous global journals and anthologies, including The Heart in Exile, Ten South African Poets, Afrika Afrika, Flora Poetica and A Century of South African Poetry.
