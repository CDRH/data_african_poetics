Biography

Viola Aloo is a poet born and raised in Cameroon, who now lives in the United States. (need birth date) She is the author of the poetry collection Bird From Africa (2015, Akashic books). Aloo was shortlisted in 2013 and 2014 for the Brunel University African Poetry Prize. She received her bachelors in psychology from the University of California Davis and a Masters degree in Anthropology for the University of Michigan Ann Arbor. She studies creative writing at American River College (get years for degrees).
