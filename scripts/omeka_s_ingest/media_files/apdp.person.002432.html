Biography

Gert Vlok Nel was born in 1963 in South Africa. He studied English, Afrikaans, and History at the University of Stellenbosch. He is the author of Om Te Lewe is Onnatuurlik (Living is Unnatural), abook exploring his youth in Beaufort-Wes, a town in the South African countryside where mainly poor whites live. It won the Ingrid Jonker Prize. This collection was followed by a CD, Beaufort Wes Se Beautiful Woorde, an autobiographical sketch which contained poems, songs, and visual material that he performed across South Africa.
