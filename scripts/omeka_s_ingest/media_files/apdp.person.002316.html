Biography

Dieudonné Mukala Kadima Nzuji was born in The Democratic Republic of Congo in 1947. He graduated with a degree in literature from Lovanium University and went on to obtain a doctorate in letters and philosophy from the University of Liège. He has written several French language poetry collections and a novel, and he has contributed significantly to the landscape of Congolese literature with his work of literary criticism ‘Littérature Zaïroise de Langue Française.’ He currently lives in Brazzaville where he is a professor of African literature at Marien Ngouabi University.
