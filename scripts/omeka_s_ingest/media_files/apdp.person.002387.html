Biography

Margaret Afuh Mbah was born in Cameroon. She is the author of four books in English, including Born Before Her Time, Fireside Stories, Flowers in the Desert, and Echoes Across Time which explore issues endemic to local Cameroonian society such as poverty and mass emigration. In addition to writing, she teaches literature and the English language at a local high school.
