Biography

Touria Majdouline was born in 1960 in Settat, Morocco. She is the author of two books of poetry, including Leaves of Ash and The Weary. She attended Mohammed V University where she received a doctorate in Modern Letters. She continued on to become a teacher of Arabic in Oujda, Morocco. She is also a member of the union of Moroccan writers, the House of Poetry in Morocco, and a founding member of the UNESCO association for creative women of the Mediterranean.
