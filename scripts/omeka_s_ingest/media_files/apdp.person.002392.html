Biography

Frida Menkan Mbunda-Nekang was born in Oku sub-division of the North West Region of Cameroon. She is the author of a book of poetry, Shadows from the Abyss, along with other plays and scholarly publications. She went to the University of Yaounde for her undergrad in English. She also received a Master's and a Doctor of Philosophy degrees at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka. She taught at the universities of Kogi State and Nsukka in Nigeria, and currently teaches at the University of Buea in Cameroon.
