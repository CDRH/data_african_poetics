Biography

Maria Eugenia Lima is an Angolan poet. Born in 1935, she studied in Angola and Portugal. She is the author of the Portuguese language books Entre a Pantera e o Esphelo, Binomio do Sangue, a book of poems, and A Cidade as Escuras, a play set in Angola in the late nineteenth century.
