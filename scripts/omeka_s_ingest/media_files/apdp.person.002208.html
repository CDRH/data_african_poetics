Biography

Vonani Bila was born in 1972 in Shirley Village, Limpopo, South Africa. Musician and poet, he is the author of several poetry collections including No Free Sleeping, co-authored with Alan Finlay and Donald Parenzee (1998), In the name of Amandla (2004) and Bilakhulu (2015). Director of the Timbila Poetry Project, he has been instrumental encouraging emerging writers and the works of marginalised poets. Bila was nominated for the DaimlerChrysler Award for Poetry in 2005. He went to Rhodes University for his Masters in Creative Writing and now teaches in the Department of English Studies at the University of Limpopo.
