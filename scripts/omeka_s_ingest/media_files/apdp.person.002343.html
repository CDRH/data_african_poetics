Biography

Monique Kwachou is a Cameroonian author and scholar. Her short stories have been published in several anthologies, and her poetry collection, Writing Therapy, was published in Cameroon by Langaa RPCIG in 2010. Kwachou is the founder and executive director of Better Breed Cameroon, a nonprofit devoted to youth development. She has also worked as a Gender Studies Instructor at University of Buea, and as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow for the Higher Education and Human Development Research Group, UFS-South Africa.
