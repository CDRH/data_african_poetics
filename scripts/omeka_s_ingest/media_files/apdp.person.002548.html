Biography

Makhosazana Xaba was born in South Africa in 1957. She is the author of several poetry collections including: The Alkalinity of Bottled Water, These Hands, and Tongues of Their Mothers. She trained as a nurse before obtaining a master's degree in creative writing from the University of Witswatersrand. She is now a professor of humanities at the University of Johannesburg. In addition to poetry, she is a short story writer. Her collection of stories, Running and Other Stories, was published in 2013 and won the 2014 Nadine Gordimer South African Literary Awards Short Story Award.
