Biography

Bewketu Seyoum was born in Mankusa, Ethiopia. He is the author of four volumes of poetry, two novels, two collections of short fiction, and several essays in Amharic. He attended Addis Ababa University where he studied psychology. He was honored as Ethiopian Best Novelist of the Year in 2008 and Best Young Author in 2009. He faced harassment because of his work at several now-banned news outlets. In 2012, he was attacked and severely beaten because of an essay he wrote about the Ethiopian saint Abune Takla Haymanot.
