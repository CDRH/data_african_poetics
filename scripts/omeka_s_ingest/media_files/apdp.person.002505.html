Biography

Thierry Sinda is a French poet of Congolose origin. He is the author of several French language books of poetry, including: “Anthologie des Poèmes d’Amour des Afriques et d’Ailleurs” (Anthology of love poems from Africa and elsewhere) and Chant du Black Paname. In addition to his literary pursuits, he is a film critic for Amina Magazine and a university professor. For the last decade, he has been building a movement of black writers called Néonégritude, a revival of the Négritude movement of the 1930s. He is also the leader of Le Printemps des Poètes d’Afrique et d’Ailleurs, a festival which brings together black poets to showcase African culture.
