Biography

Mbuyiseni Oswald Mtshali was born in 1940 in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. He is the author of several poetry collections including Sounds of a Cowhide Drum and Fireflames. After apartheid legislation prevented him from studying, he obtained a diploma from the Premier School of Journalism and Authorship, affiliated with London University. He went on to receive an undergraduate degree in the New School of Social Research, an MFA from Columbia University, and a Doctor of Philosophy from Columbia University. He went on to teach at the New York City College of Technology until his return to South Africa in 2007.
